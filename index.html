About this pattern calculator

The Knitwear Studio pattern calculator is an AI-assisted tool built to help knitters of all levels generate accurate, construction-aware sweater patterns from scratch. The calculator supports three sweater constructions: drop shoulder (Top-down, back-first, drop shoulder-style with shoulder short rows and waist shaping), raglan (top-down seamless with 5-pair German Short Row neck shaping and body-only increase rounds for larger sizes), and set-in sleeves (flat and seamed, suitable for structured garments).

Every pattern adapts to your gauge automatically — whether you are working in chunky yarn at 3 stitches per inch or fine lace weight at 8 stitches per 10 cm. Ease is calculated natively in your chosen unit: inches or centimetres. Stitch patterns supported include garter, 1×1 rib, and cable (with automatic 10% draw-in compensation).

The construction logic is modelled on professional knitwear design principles, including progressive short row neck shaping inspired by published raglan patterns, and sloped shoulder shaping inspired by seamless drop shoulder patterns from leading independent designers. This calculator is ideal for knitters who want to move beyond standard sizes and create garments that fit their actual body.
